The honest part · firm is not a miracle

A disciplined session still can't
invent ground truth.

No runbooks, stale comments, tribal knowledge → firm reinforces the confidence, not the truth. Its leverage is real only where there's structured documentation and tooling it can reach. What it does do is make the missing ground visible — often the first reason a team fills it in.
