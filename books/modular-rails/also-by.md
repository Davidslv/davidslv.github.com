<h1 id="also-by-david-silva">Also by David Silva</h1>

<p><strong>Building Your Own Roguelike: A Practical Guide</strong><br />
<em>Master Roguelike Game Development in Ruby</em></p>

<p>A hands-on guide to building a roguelike game from scratch in pure Ruby — no game engine, no frameworks. Covers procedural generation, Entity-Component-System architecture, event-driven design, and pathfinding algorithms. If you enjoy building things from first principles, this is the book for you.</p>
